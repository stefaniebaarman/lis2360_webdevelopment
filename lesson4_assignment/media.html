Influential People in Technology

Elon Musk Elon Musk created Tesla, the most high tech car to date. His company is now worth over $50 billion and he continues use his knowledge on artificial intelligence to create amazing technologies.
Mark Zuckerburg Mark Zuckerburg created Facebook which opened up a whole new world for our generation. We are now able to keep up with what our friends and family are doing and communicate with others even if they are half way across the world.
Jeff Bezos Jeff Bezos is the founder and CEO of Amazon.com, the most popular online shopping website. Now, whichever product we want, we can have it at our doorstep within a matter of days. Amazon has made online shopping cheap, safe, and efficient.
